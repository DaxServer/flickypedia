Flickypedia bookmarklet

A bookmarklet is a browser bookmark that does something extra.

Use the Flickypedia bookmark as you’re exploring Flickr to quickly send the URL of a Flickr page straight to Flickypedia. It’s easy!

Drag the button below to your menu bar to install your Flickypedia bookmarklet today!

And remember:
Flickypedia will filter any Flickr URL you submit for photos that match the Wikimedia Commons rules. That means it only shows public and safe Flickr photos licensed with either CC BY, CC BY‑SA, CC0, or Public Domain.
